About Us

Born from a Passion for Beauty

Glam by Erriane was founded in 2026 by makeup artist Erriane Manalo, who believed that great makeup should be accessible to everyone — not just the privileged few.

We source only cruelty-free, dermatologist-tested products and partner with Filipino artisans to celebrate local beauty culture. Every product in our store is personally tested by our team before it reaches your door.

We believe makeup is a form of self-expression — and our mission is to give every person the tools to express themselves boldly and beautifully.
